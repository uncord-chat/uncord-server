Server Rules

Welcome! To keep this community enjoyable for everyone, please follow these rules.

1. Be Respectful

Treat all members with courtesy and respect. Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, and personal attacks are not tolerated. Disagree with ideas, not people.

2. No Spam or Self-Promotion

Do not flood channels with repetitive messages, unsolicited advertisements, or promotional content. Share links and resources only when they are relevant to the conversation.

3. Keep Content Appropriate

Do not post content that is explicit, violent, or otherwise inappropriate. Use content warnings when discussing sensitive topics and always respect channel-specific guidelines.

4. Respect Privacy

Do not share personal information about other members without their consent. This includes real names, locations, photos, and private messages.

5. Use Channels Appropriately

Post messages in the channel that best fits the topic. Read channel descriptions before posting and keep off-topic conversations in designated channels.

6. Follow Moderator Instructions

Moderators are here to keep the community safe and welcoming. Follow their guidance and use the appropriate channels to raise concerns or appeals.

7. No Impersonation

Do not impersonate other members, moderators, or public figures. Use a username and profile that accurately represent you.

These rules may be updated at any time. Continued participation implies acceptance of the current rules.
